Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to DevHelpers! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage.

No Data Collection

DevHelpers does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. The app provides tools for developers, including the ability to scan barcodes using the camera. However, any images captured for barcode scanning are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Camera Usage

The app uses your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes. The images captured during this process are stored locally on your device. DevHelpers does not collect or upload these images to any backend server.

Changes to This Policy

Although we currently do not collect any data, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect any changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@devhelpers.com.
